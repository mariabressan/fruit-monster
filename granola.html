GRANOLA!

  • 3.5 cups oldfashioned oats
  • 1 cup nuts (almond, pumpkin seed :)
  • make simple syrup: 1/4 tsp salt + 1/2 cup granulated sugar dissolved in 1/2 cup water (microwave a minute)
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • Combine dry and wet ingredients
  • Mix and spread out on a baking sheet
  • Bake at 250 F for 2 hours!
