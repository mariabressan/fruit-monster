SOURDOUGH BREAD!

  • Day 1: Feed sourdough in the morning, doubling the amount, take out half, cover in a bowl and set in a warm spot (3/4 cup)
  • In the evening, mix the flour:
    • 12 oz all porpose
    • 6 oz whole wheat
    • 1 cup mixed grains (2 tbsp each - sesame, flax, almond, rye flour, quick oats, quinoa, chia)
    • 2 cups room temp water, microwave 90 seconds, dissolve:
      • 2 tbsp brown sugar
      • 2 tsp salt
    • Autolyse:
      • pour water in flour mix, stir until combined
      • let sit for 30 min
    • Drizzle olive oli (3tbsp)
    • Pour start over
    • Mix with hand, folding is optional (3-4 folds, 20 min interval)
    • Cover with a shower cap, let sit overnight
  • The next morning:
    • Flour surfaces generously
    • Pour out dough mixture, fold a couple times
    • Let sit in proofing basket, smooth side up (about 2 hrs)
  • 45 min before baking:
    • Put cast iron dutch oven in oven with lid, preheat to 450 F
    • Carefully pour dough into iron (use extra flour if sticky)
    • Bake 35 min closed, 15 min open
