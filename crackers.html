YUMMY QUACKA!

Ingredients:

  • some flower, maybe a mix of brown black purple, whatever color youre feeling
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil per batch you wanna make
  • a chick of black pepper
  • some water, until the consistency is a-okay
  • anything else you like, sesame street, rosemary, time
Steps:
  1. mixido all the ingredientosos
  2. let it sit for an hour to make it easier to work with. If the dough is bored, maybe put on some netflix or play some music for it
  3. separate into good-sized chinks, maybe what a dinasour egg size would be if it were pretty big
  4. turn into Nonna Maria, and whip out that pasta machine!
  5. roll and roll till the 4th thickest layer
  6. cut into a cool unconventional oven shape
  7. bake at 350F for 12-14 minutes, turn it around halfway if you like
  8. I AM YOUR CONSCIENCE YOU MUST EAT AND EAT AND EAT AND EAT
